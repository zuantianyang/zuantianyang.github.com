title: "CentOS下在登录界面不显示用户名"
tags: [linux]
---

修改/etc/gdm/gdm.schemas,找到greeter/Exclude部分，加入登录界面需要隐藏的用户名，保存退出！

<code>
<schema>
  <key>greeter/Exclude</key>
  <signature>s</signature>
  <default>zuantianyang,bin,root,daemon,adm,lp,sync,shutdown,halt,mail,news,uucp,operator,
nobody,nobody4,noaccess,postgres,pvm,rpm,nfsnobody,pcap</default>
</schema>
</code>
